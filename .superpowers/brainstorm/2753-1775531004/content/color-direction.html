What visual mood should the website have?

Wolli Creek Cultural Tours — choose the colour palette and atmosphere that feels right

DEEP EARTH OCHRE WARM AMBER SANDSTONE

A — Country & Earth

Deep chocolate browns, rich ochres, and warm amber — colours drawn directly from Wolli Creek's sandstone, soil and bark. Bold and grounded, evoking deep connection to Country. High contrast, earthy warmth.

FOREST CREEK BUSH PAPERBARK

B — Bush & Water

Deep forest greens through to soft paperbark — a palette inspired by the creek corridor, bush regeneration, and native canopy. Calming, ecological, and lush. Speaks to the living landscape.

IRONBARK TERRACOTTA VALLEY GREEN WHEAT

C — Earth & Valley (Blended)

Terracotta and ironbark from the soil, accented by valley green from the bush corridor — the full spectrum of Wolli Creek's landscape. Warm, balanced, and layered. Best of both worlds.
